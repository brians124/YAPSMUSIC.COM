Our Journey

The YAPS Story

From a small studio dream to a global music platform empowering thousands of artists across Africa and beyond.

🎙️
2020
The Beginning
YAPS MUSIC was founded with a single mission — give upcoming African artists a platform to share their talent with the world without barriers.
2021
First 1,000 Artists
We hit our first major milestone — 1,000 registered artists across 8 countries, with over 10,000 tracks uploaded to the platform.
2022
Global Expansion
YAPS MUSIC expanded to 20+ countries, launching our artist promotion program that helped hundreds of creators grow their fanbase.
2024
1 Million Listeners
We proudly crossed 1 million active listeners — proving that authentic, independent music resonates across cultures and continents.
NOW
The Future is Sound
With new features, artist monetization, and live sessions rolling out — the best chapter of YAPS MUSIC is just beginning.
